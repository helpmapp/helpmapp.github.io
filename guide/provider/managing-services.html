Managing Services ​

Once you have claimed your service, you can update its details from the Provider Dashboard.

What You Can Update ​

  • Opening Hours: Keep your schedule accurate.
  • Services Offered: Tag specific services like "Hot Meal", "Shower", or "Legal Advice".
  • Eligibility: Specify if your service is for specific groups (e.g., Youth, Women Only).
  • Contact Info: Update phone numbers and website links.

Real-Time Updates ​

Changes you make are reflected on the app immediately, helping thousands of users find the right support.
