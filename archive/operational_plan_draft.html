HelpmApp Operational Plan (Reset Edition) ​

Version: 2.0
Last Updated: 17 Nov 2025
Scope: 18-month roadmap aligned with clean-slate rebuild

1. Guiding Principles ​

  1. Build-small, prove-fast: Ship a lean MVP with automated data ingest and analytics hooks before adding advanced features.
  2. Lived-experience co-design: Embed peer advocates and service partners into each phase for continuous validation.
  3. Data & rewards first: Engagement incentives and trustworthy service data are non-negotiable launch requirements.
  4. Transparent reset narrative: Keep partners and funders updated monthly on rebuild progress, blockers, and learnings.

2. Phased Roadmap (18 Months) ​

PhaseMonthsPrimary GoalsSuccess Metrics
Foundation1-6Rebuild MVP stack, restart user/provider acquisition in 3 pilot boroughs, launch rewards v1500 active users, 50 providers (10 premium), £10k rewards value
Growth7-12Expand across London, ship dashboards v1, secure borough pilots & sponsor base2,000 users, 100 providers, 3 borough pilots, 7 sponsors
Scale13-18Enter Manchester/Birmingham, launch native apps & API, mature analytics5,000 users, 200 providers, 360k check-ins, £200k public-sector pipeline

7. Reporting Cadence ​

  • Weekly: Rebuild stand-up summary (engineering + product).
  • Monthly: Partner bulletin + public changelog.
  • Quarterly: Board/advisory update including KPIs, risks, funding pipeline.
  • Ad hoc: Incident or milestone reports (e.g., data breach drill, sponsor announcements).

8. Immediate Next Steps ​

  1. Finalise MVP architecture RFC and circulate to advisors for review.

  4. Prepare grant application packs with refreshed market data and KPIs.
