HelpmApp Financial Projections (Reset Edition) ​

Version: 2.0
Last Updated: 17 Nov 2025
Status: Core product being rebuilt; projections assume relaunch Month 3 post-reset

1. Modelling Approach ​

  • Focused on operational economics; engineering rebuild funded separately by founders/grants-in-kind.
  • 36-month horizon starting Month 0 = reset kickoff.
  • Conservative case used for grant applications; upside scenario kept in appendix (not yet published).
  • Revenue recognised only after contractual sign-off; sponsorship in-kind tracked but excluded from P&L except where cash equivalent offsets rewards spend.

6. Unit Economics (Year 2 steady state) ​

  • CAC (blended): £45 per active user (marketing + outreach / new users per year).
  • Rewards cost per active user: ~£27/year (based on redemption + sponsor mix).
  • LTV (Year 2+): £180/user (assuming 24-month relationship, mix of sponsorship + LA revenue share attributable per user).
  • Provider ARPU: £1,440/year (premium) vs £0 (basic). Target premium penetration 30-40% by Year 3.
