HelpmApp Funding Research Notes ​

1. UK Grants & Social Impact Funds ​

DSIT Digital Inclusion Innovation Fund ​

  • Status: Closed to applications as of Sep 2025, but monitoring for 2026 cycles is essential.
  • Focus: Innovative interventions and best practice to increase digital participation.
  • Grants: £25,000 to £500,000.
  • Relevance: High. HelpmApp fits the "innovative intervention" and "best practice" criteria perfectly.

London Homelessness Foundation (LHF) ​

  • Status: Active.
  • Focus: Early-stage, higher-risk projects requiring proof of concept.
  • Grants: Typically £20,000 - £30,000 for new projects.
  • Relevance: Very High. HelpmApp is a London-based agency working directly with homeless people.

Bethnal Green Ventures (BGV) - Tech for Good ​

  • Status: Applications open for Spring 2026 (Deadline: 4 Jan 2026).
  • Offer: £60,000 investment for 7% equity + 6-week accelerator.
  • Criteria: Innovative tech, scalability, intentionality, direct impact.
  • Relevance: High for scaling, but requires "Company Limited by Shares" structure (may conflict with CIC if not structured as a subsidiary).

2. Borough-Specific & Local Innovation ​

Westminster City Council - DigitALL Project ​

  • Status: Active (Nov 2024).
  • Focus: Digital inclusion sessions.
  • Funding: £15,000 shared among partners.
  • Relevance: Medium. Good for localized pilot funding.

Camden Council - Rough Sleeping Support ​

  • Status: Active (Oct 2025).
  • Funding: £680k boost for support services.
  • Relevance: High. HelpmApp can position itself as a digital layer for these services.

3. Corporate CSR & Sponsorship ​

Slaughter and May ​

  • Focus: Helping people experiencing homelessness in London.
  • Partner: Single Homeless Project (SHP).
  • Opportunity: Potential for pro-bono legal support or direct sponsorship of the rewards pool.

Fitzrovia Partnership ​

  • Focus: Supporting homeless and vulnerable people in London (Fitzrovia).
  • Opportunity: Localized sponsorship for rewards in the Central London area.

Crisis / Centrepoint / St Mungo's ​

  • Opportunity: These major charities have corporate partnership teams. HelpmApp can partner with them to provide the digital "check-in" and rewards layer for their existing service users.
